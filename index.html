The loop

One rental car, picked up and dropped off at Jeju International Airport, does the whole trip. We go clockwise so there's no backtracking: North → East → South → West → North. No single drive is longer than about 90 minutes.

Before you fly

Do first

Get a 1949-convention International Driving Permit

Apply in person at AA Singapore before you leave — it can't be issued in Korea or emailed. Bring it plus your passport and Singapore licence to the rental counter. See Gotchas.

Good news

No visa, no K-ETA, no leave-of-absence

Singapore passports are K-ETA/visa-exempt for Korea, and Nov 21 is the day after MOE's 2026 school year ends (Fri 20 Nov) — this trip shouldn't need a leave-of-absence application.
