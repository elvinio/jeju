What the S$2,637 buffer is for

This plan is deliberately not stretched to the cap, because flights and resort rates are estimates until you actually book them — a fare spike or a peak-rate weekend could easily eat several hundred dollars. Keeping ~29% in reserve means one of those surprises doesn't blow the budget. If prices come in as estimated, here's where the leftover could go:

  • Upgrade the Jungmun splurge to a true 5-star (Shilla, Parnas) or extend it a night +S$300–600
  • Seat selection / extra baggage on the flights +S$150–300
  • Add a private or small-group guided day (e.g. Hallasan lower-trail hike with a guide) +S$300–500
  • Bigger shopping/duty-free allowance flexible
  • Just keep it as contingency — recommended given how far out these estimates are S$2,637
