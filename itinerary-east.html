Option A · Day by day

North & East, unhurried

Four nights in the green north, then four in the east — a single move, both stays a hotel or resort. The pace is deliberately loose: forests, a grassy crater, the island's most famous sunrise, and a whole slow day on car-free Udo. Nothing here is more than about an hour's drive. Late November days are short (sunrise ~07:20, sunset ~17:25), so plans wrap by dusk and dinners are early.

Singapore Changi → Jeju Int'l (CJU), direct, ~6h. Likely an afternoon/evening arrival.
On landing
Clear immigration under Jeju's direct-entry visa waiver (no visa/K-ETA needed for Singapore passports). Collect the rental car — bring IDP, passport and SG licence; confirm the 2 booster seats are fitted. The north resort is only 10–20 min from the airport.
Evening
Check in and stay put. Easy dinner nearby — Dongmun Market or a black pork restaurant — then an early night to recover from the flight.

Base: East (Seongsan), night 1 of 4
26
Thu
EastSlow day
A whole day on Udo Island
Late morning
Short ferry from Seongsan Port to Udo (leave the rental car at the port — cars can't cross for a day visit). No need for the first sailing; this is an amble, not a mission.
On the island
Rent an e-bike or family cart and loop the island at walking speed: Seobin Baeksa's white-coral beach, Hagosudong beach, the peanut-ice-cream stops, and quiet lighthouse views.
Evening
Last ferry back is around 17:30. Dinner in Seongsan, early night.
Base: East (Seongsan), night 2 of 4
27
Fri
EastPeak + coast
Sunrise Peak & Seopjikoji
Morning
Climb Seongsan Ilchulbong (Sunrise Peak) — a 30–45 min walk up a tuff-cone crater rim. You needn't do the literal sunrise; a mid-morning climb beats the tour buses and is warmer for the kids. Prefer flat? The Gwangchigi tidal flats below give the same view with no climb.
Afternoon
Seopjikoji — a gentle clifftop coastal walk past a lighthouse and canola fields — then Aqua Planet Jeju aquarium right next door, a reliable indoor backup if the wind picks up.
Evening
Back to the resort pool, easy dinner.
